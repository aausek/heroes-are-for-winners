BIO

The iconic and imcomparable Dutch DJ Armin van Buuren has been in the electronic music scene for years bringing euphoria to listeners from all over the world. AvB's tracks vary from deep trance to dance and he fails to disappoint with each new releasead album or live set.

AvB has hosted the weekly radio show A State of Trance since 2001 and the program has grown immensely in the past decade due to increased exposure and popularity of electronic music. van Buuren has in average 37 million listeners per aired show.
